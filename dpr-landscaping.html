DPR & LAND­SCAPING

Detailed Project Reports, master-planning, and landscape design — the upstream work that makes downstream construction and interiors run on time, on budget, and on intent.

01

DETAILED
PROJECT REPORTS

A bankable DPR pulls together everything a project needs to break ground — site analysis, design intent, structural & MEP scope, BOQs, costing, and phased delivery schedule. We write DPRs that read clearly to engineers, financiers, and approving authorities alike.

  • Site analysis & feasibility study
  • Architectural & engineering scope
  • Bill of quantities (BOQ) & cost estimation
  • Phased construction schedule
  • Regulatory drawings & approval packets
02

LANDSCAPE
DESIGN

Landscape isn’t decoration — it’s the part of the project that ages best when done right. We design gardens, courtyards, terraces, and resort landscapes that work with Nepal’s climate, local planting, and the way owners actually use outdoor space.

  • Residential gardens & courtyards
  • Hospitality grounds & resort masterplans
  • Hardscape, drainage & lighting design
  • Native & climate-appropriate planting plans
  • Water features, pergolas & outdoor rooms
Process

HOW WE WORK

01   BRIEF

Site visit, stakeholder interviews, and a clear written brief before any drawing starts.

02   CONCEPT

Massing studies, landscape concepts, and 2–3 design directions for owner review.

03   DEVELOPMENT

Detail design, material schedules, planting plans, and costing aligned to budget.

04   DPR

Full DPR documentation packaged for approvals, financing, and construction tendering.

05   OVERSIGHT

Site supervision during execution to keep design intent intact through delivery.

06   HANDOVER

As-built drawings, maintenance manual, and a one-year garden establishment plan.
