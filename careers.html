SENIOR ARCHITECT · DESIGNER

Full-Time
Baluwatar, Kathmandu 5+ Years Experience Open Roll

Lead architectural design from concept through DPR — residential, hospitality and commercial. You’ll own end-to-end project responsibility from site visit to handover, working directly with principals on every major design decision.

Key Responsibilities
  • Lead design from concept & massing through DPR documentation
  • Develop architectural drawings in AutoCAD, SketchUp and/or Revit
  • Coordinate structural, MEP and contractor inputs on running projects
  • Present design to clients and manage approvals through Nepali byelaws
  • Mentor junior designers and review their drawings before issue
What We Look For
  • 5+ years of practice in Nepal or comparable context
  • Strong portfolio of completed residential or hospitality projects
  • Working knowledge of local building byelaws & approvals
  • Field experience supervising contractors and finishing trades
  • Bachelor’s in Architecture (B.Arch) from a recognised institution

SENIOR INTERIOR DESIGNER

Full-Time
Baluwatar, Kathmandu 5+ Years Experience Open Roll

Own interior projects end-to-end — concept boards, material specification, FF&E sourcing, vendor management and on-site execution. Hospitality and high-end residential experience preferred.

Key Responsibilities
  • Develop concept boards, mood & material palettes for client review
  • Produce interior drawings: layouts, elevations, RCPs, joinery details
  • Specify FF&E — source furniture, fixtures, finishes & manage vendors
  • Run client presentations and design approval cycles
  • Supervise site execution, finishing trades and styling at handover
What We Look For
  • 5+ years of interior design practice with completed projects
  • Strong portfolio — hospitality, premium residential or commercial
  • Fluent with SketchUp, AutoCAD; mood-boarding in Photoshop/InDesign
  • Comfortable presenting design to clients and negotiating with vendors
  • Bachelor’s in Interior Design or Architecture
