Commercial Design in Kathmandu, Nepal

NextGen works as a full-service commercial building designer in Kathmandu, shaping spaces that perform for business. We design and deliver offices, retail stores, showrooms, restaurants, cafes and mixed-use developments across Nepal.

Our restaurant and cafe design balances brand identity, customer flow and operational efficiency, while our office projects create modern, productive workplaces. Every commercial fit-out is planned around footfall, function and a finish that lasts in high-traffic environments.
